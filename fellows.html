Introduction

About IncubateIND Fellowship

INCIND Technology Fellowship is a unique and exclusive program for the coders, hackers, designers and innovators of tomorrow. As part of this fellowship, you will get an opportunity to develop your skills through working with senior technology leaders and industry experts.

Over the years, a lot of talented students seeking opportunities to sharpen their skills have joined IncubateIND to get to work with industry leaders and to understand the complexities of the tech world. This Fellowship will make students work with technology leaders, and it aims to improve the chances of a few selected students to get hired by their dream organizations in the future.

Upcoming Events

For Students

  • Interaction and Exposure: Fellows would get to participate in and publicize various technology based events, this will help gain exposure.
  • Skills: Fellows get opportunities to sharpen and develop their professional skills.
  • Certificates and letters: Fellows get certificates and recommendation letters to enhance their resumes.
  • Internship opportunities: Priority access of internship opportunities with top tech startups.
    • Direct Entry into IncubateIND’s hackathons: Selected fellows who fare well would get a direct entry into some of IncIND’s top hackathons.
  • Event Support: Fellows can get to host workshops and meet-ups in their college on a regular basis, we could also help to get in touch with speakers for those workshops. Goodies and swags can be provided.*
